Policy Text v1.2

Required

1. Data Collection

Acme Industries collects a mathematical "face template" derived from your facial features. We do not store high-resolution photographs of your face on our servers.

2. Purpose of Usage

This biometric template is used exclusively for attendance verification and to prevent "buddy punching." It ensures that only authorized personnel can log entry into secure facilities.

3. Retention & Security

Your biometric data is encrypted using AES-256 standards. Retention is limited to the duration of your employment plus 30 days, or until consent is withdrawn. Data is never shared with third parties for marketing.

4. Compliance

This policy adheres to local data protection regulations including GDPR and BIPA requirements for biometric privacy and disclosure.
